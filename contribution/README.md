# The Provenance Manifesto

The **Provenance Manifesto** explores how we design, build, and govern systems in the age of artificial intelligence by preserving the provenance of the decisions that shape them.

Modern software systems are no longer only written by humans. They emerge from a continuous interaction between engineers, organizations, and increasingly autonomous AI systems. Without traceable decisions and institutional memory, systems become difficult to understand, govern, and evolve.

The Provenance Manifesto proposes a simple shift:

**Decisions should be treated as first-class artifacts of software development.**
