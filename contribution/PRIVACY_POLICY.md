# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Introduction

ProvenanceManifesto.org ("we", "our", or "the site") is a public informational website presenting the **Provenance Manifesto** and related materials.

We respect your privacy and aim to collect **as little personal data as possible**.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit the site and how it is used.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Website Access Data

When you visit this website, basic technical information may be processed automatically by the hosting infrastructure (such as GitHub Pages).

This may include:

- IP address
- browser type
- operating system
- referring page
- date and time of access

This information is processed solely for the purpose of operating and securing the website.

We do not use this information to personally identify visitors.

---

### 2. Manifesto Signatures (if available)

If the website provides the ability to **sign the Provenance Manifesto**, limited information may be collected in order to record the signature.

This may include:

- name or chosen display name
- timestamp of the signature
- optional affiliation or organization (if provided)

To reduce exposure of personal data, signatures may be stored in **obfuscated form**, for example by masking characters in names.

Providing this information is voluntary.

---

### 3. Contributions

If you contribute to the manifesto through platforms such as GitHub (for example through issues, discussions, or pull requests), your information is processed according to the privacy policies of those platforms.

---

## Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies.

However, third-party services that may be linked from the site (such as GitHub) may use cookies according to their own privacy policies.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Data may be processed by infrastructure providers necessary to operate the website, such as:

- GitHub Pages (hosting)
- GitHub (repository and collaboration platform)

---

## Data Retention

Any collected data is retained only for as long as necessary to operate the website and maintain the historical record of the manifesto.

Manifesto signatures may be retained as part of the project's public history.

---

## External Links

This site may contain links to external websites.  
We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the website or legal requirements.

The latest version will always be available on this page.

---

## Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the Provenance Manifesto project, please contact the project maintainer through the project's official repository or website.
