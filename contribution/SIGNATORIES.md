
# Signatories of the Provenance Manifesto

Individuals who support the principles of the Provenance Manifesto may publicly sign it.

Signing the manifesto indicates agreement with its core ideas and support for improving transparency, traceability, and governance of decisions in modern systems.

Signing does **not** imply responsibility for maintaining the document.

---

## How to Sign

To add your name to the list of signatories:

1. Fork the repository
2. Add your name to the list below
3. Submit a Pull Request

You may optionally include:

- Organization
- Role
- Country
- Website or LinkedIn

Example entry:

```
Jane Doe — Software Architect, Example Corp (Germany)
https://linkedin.com/in/janedoe
```

Maintainers will review and merge signatory requests.
